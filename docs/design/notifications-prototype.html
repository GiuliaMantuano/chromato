Brandable vs OS-fixed

  • Brandable: app icon, content image, title & body copy, app name, sound, action buttons (terminal-notifier only)
  • OS-fixed: card shape & corner radius, blur/translucency, fonts, text colours, light/dark styling, position, dismiss/stack behaviour
  • ⚠️ Today chromato uses osascript display notification → no custom icon (borrows the terminal's icon) and no buttons. Branding the icon needs a delivery switch (terminal-notifier / node-notifier — already a dependency).
