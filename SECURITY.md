# Security Policy

| Version | Supported |
|---------|-----------|
| v1.0.x  | ✅ Supported |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please use GitHub's private vulnerability reporting feature to disclose security issues responsibly.

To report a vulnerability:

1. Go to the **Security** tab of this repository.
2. Click **"Report a vulnerability"**.
3. Fill in the details of the issue.

Do not disclose security vulnerabilities publicly until they have been reviewed and addressed. We will acknowledge your report within 5 business days and aim to release a fix within 30 days for confirmed vulnerabilities.
